About Rangoli Properties

Built on Trust,
Rooted in Braj

Rangoli Properties has grown into one of the fastest-moving real estate consultancies in the Braj region — working across residential plots, luxury villas, flats, farmhouses, agricultural land and commercial properties in Mathura, Vrindavan, Barsana, Govardhan and the towns around them.

Every deal we close rests on the same three things: a clean title, complete documentation, and honest advice about what a property is actually worth. That approach is why homebuyers, investors and developers in this region keep coming back to us.

We don't disappear after the token amount is paid. From the first site visit to the final registration — and the loan paperwork in between — our team stays with you.
